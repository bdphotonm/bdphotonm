Common Questions

FAQ

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes! I'm based in New Mexico but available for travel throughout the Southwest and beyond. Travel fees apply for shoots more than 50 miles from Albuquerque — contact me for details.

How do I book a session?

Send me a message through the contact page or email me directly. Sessions are held with a 50% deposit at booking, with the remainder due the day of the shoot.

What if the weather is bad?

Outdoor sessions can be rescheduled at no charge if weather makes shooting unsafe or impractical. We'll pick a new date that works for both of us.

How long until I receive my photos?

Portrait sessions are typically delivered within 5–7 business days. Real estate photography is delivered within 24 hours. Rush delivery is available — contact me for pricing.

Can I request specific shots or styles?

Absolutely. I encourage clients to share inspiration images before the shoot so we're on the same page. That said, I always leave room for happy accidents — sometimes the best shots are unplanned.
